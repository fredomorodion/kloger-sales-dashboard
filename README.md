# 📊 Kroger Sales Dashboard

![Dashboard Preview](kloger final dashboard screenshot.png)

---

## 🔍 Overview
This project analyzes sales and profitability using the Kroger Superstore dataset.

The goal was to identify key revenue drivers, regional performance patterns, and customer behavior through a structured dashboard.

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- The West region leads in total revenue, showing uneven regional performance  
- California dominates sales, indicating strong but concentrated growth  
- Technology is the top-performing category, driving the highest revenue  
- Customers prefer Standard Class shipping, showing cost sensitivity  
- Sub-categories like Phones and Chairs contribute significantly to revenue  

---

## 🎯 Business Impact
- Highlights opportunities to grow underperforming regions  
- Reveals dependency on high-performing states  
- Identifies top product categories for strategic focus  
- Supports better logistics and shipping decisions
